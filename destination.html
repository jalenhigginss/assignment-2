Travelogue: Pensacola, Florida

Overview

Pensacola pairs sugar‑white Gulf beaches with a walkable, brick‑lined downtown anchored by Palafox Street. A wonderful place to have fun with your family and friends especially if you have family in Florida. Come for emerald water and sunset piers; stay for seafood shacks, live music, and easy day trips to Gulf Islands National Seashore.

Must-See Spots

  1. Pensacola Beach & Gulf Islands National Seashore (Fort Pickens)
  2. Historic Pensacola Village
  3. National Naval Aviation Museum
  4. Palafox Street & Downtown Pensacola
  5. Pensacola Lighthouse & Maritime Museum

Visitor Tips

  • Florida sun is intense! Pack reef‑safe sunscreen, a hat, and plenty of water.
  • Check the Blue Angels practice schedule; arrive early for parking near viewing areas.
  • Beach traffic can back up on bridges; use trolleys/shuttles when available.
  • Swim only when beach flags indicate safe conditions and respect marked sea‑turtle nests.
